The first floor of Entrepôt du Congo is on on Friday, Saturday and Sunday during the evening a restaurant. We can possibly reserve this room for groups from 20 persons and/or at least €500 in consummations.
On the other days and evenings the space can also be reserved, we ask for this no charge if at least for €200 is consummated, if not the rental price is €15 an hour.

A projection screen and flipover boards are freely available. Service is included, the prices are the same as in the café downstairs.

For groups with at least 20 people we suggest the Italian buffet, inspired on the regional Italian cuisine. It consists of 40 dishes, half hot, half cold. The price for the buffet, without drinks and dessert, is €30 per person.

Up to 15 people can eat à la carte.

Too make reservations contact +32 475 52 82 15 or info@entrepotducongo.com.
Payment is possible using Visa, Mastercard and Cash. A ticket, credit card receipt and VAT receipt is supplied as offical payment document, a invoice is not supplied.
