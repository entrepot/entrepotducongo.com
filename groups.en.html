The first floor of Entrepôt du Congo is on on Thursday, Friday, Saturday and Sunday during the evening a restaurant. We can possibly reserve this room for groups from 20 persons and/or at least €500 in consummations.
On the other days and evenings the space can also be reserved, we ask for this no charge if at least for €200 is consummated, if not the rental price is €15 an hour.

A projection screen and flipover boards are freely available. Service is included, the prices are the same as in the café downstairs.

For groups over 20 people we suggest the Italian buffet. It consists of around 40 different kind of dishes, half are hot, half cold. The price for a buffet without drinks and dessert is €24 a person.

Payment is possible with Visa/Mastercard or cash.
