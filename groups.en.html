The first floor of l'Entrepôt du Congo can be rented for receptions, castings, babyshowers, dinners, ... Parties are not possible, due to restrictions in sound.

The rentalprice is €15 per hour, unless for more than €500 is consumed. Service is included, the prices are the same as in the café downstairs.
A projection screen and flipover boards are freely available.

For groups with at least 30 people we suggest the Italian buffet, inspired on the regional Italian cuisine. It consists of around fifty dishes, half hot, half cold. The price for the buffet, without drinks and dessert, is €35 per person. A minimum of 30 people will always be charged, even if for example only 23 people are present.
Another possiblity are our choices of group dishes and from the extensive menu from downstairs.
Up to 15 people can eat à la carte.

Drinks can be ordered in the café downstairs.
It's possible to use your own wines (for €10 a bottle), and bubbles (for €15 a bottle) and destilled drinks (for €15 a bottle).

The kitchen on the first floor can be used, it costs €15/hour. There is no dishwashingmachine.

Too make reservations contact +32 475 52 82 15 or info@entrepotducongo.com.
Payment is possible using Visa, Mastercard and Cash. A ticket, credit card receipt and VAT receipt is supplied as offical payment document, a invoice is not supplied.
